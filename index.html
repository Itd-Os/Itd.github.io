Security & Privacy

Your privacy is our priority. This year we have decided to integrate firefox with tracking protection and a new firewall to Itd Os X to protect your computer from the possibility of being hacked. We have also restricted privileged accesses with SELinux and AppArmor. In terms of security we have added the antivirus Clamtk a totally free and open source antivirus that guarantees greater reliability and security

Be Productive

Thanks to the libreoffice suite and the Joplin notes app you can be more productive than ever we take Joplin besides taking notes you can create to-do lists, code samples, copy entire web pages or just their content you can also import notes from Evernote and you can you choose how to synchronize your data so that it is yours only via Nextcloud, Dropbox, OneDrive and WebDAV.

Google-Assistant now on Itd Os

Thanks to Melvin. L. Abraham and his project on GitHub: "Google-Assistant-Unofficial-Desktop-Client" we are now able to bring the voice assistant that we all love and enjoy on Itd Os ensuring better integration with the Google ecosystem
